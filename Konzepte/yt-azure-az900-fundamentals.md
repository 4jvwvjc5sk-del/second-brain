- Cloud-Konzepte (CAPEX/OPEX, HA, Skalierung, Disaster Recovery)
- Azure-Kernservices (Compute, Storage, Networking)
- Sicherheit & Identity (Entra ID, RBAC, Zero Trust, Key Vault)
- Abrechnung & Governance (SLA, TCO, Blueprints, Purview)
- Management-Tools (Portal, CLI, ARM Templates, Terraform, Bicep)
